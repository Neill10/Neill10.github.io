So what are the DrawBacks?

Unfortunately, with as many benefits that AI brings to the table, there is also a myriad of problems that arise from the use of this technology.

Ethic

The major drawback of using of AI is how unethical it can be. Since AI can self learn to achieve satisfying results, companies will choose them over human laborers This in turn, will lead to the loss of many jobs.
Not only that but the current use of AI invades our privacy. As I've touched upon before, in order for an AI program to learn by itself, it has to take in a large amount of data. That doesn't really raise any red flags...until you realize that some of these data can be sensitive personal information. That runs the risk of leaking terabytes of data to the world.
